Banana Bread

Description

Banana bread is a type of bread made from mashed bananas.

It is often a moist, sweet; however there are some banana bread recipes that are yeast raised breads.

Ingredients

  1. 3 ripe bananas
  2. 1/2 cup of unsalted butter
  3. 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  4. 2 large eggs
  5. 1 1/2 cups of all-purpose flour
  6. 1 tsp baking soda
  7. 1/2 tsp salt
  8. 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
  9. 1 cup walnuts
