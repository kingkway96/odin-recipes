Rasta Pasta

Description

Rasta pasta is creamy pasta tossed with jerk chicken and bell peppers.

This dish is so full of flavor, and makes a great weeknight dinner.

Ingredients

  1. Chicken
  2. Bell Peppers
  3. Pasta

  5. Jerk Seasoning
